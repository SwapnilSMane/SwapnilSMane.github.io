Swapnil Mane is a PMRF research scholar in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur (IIT Jodhpur). He is working in the research group SoNAA: Social Network Analysis and Application led by Dr. Suman Kundu, Assistant Professor at IIT, Jodhpur. The research group is part of the Cognitive and Social Analytics Lab (CSA Lab), affiliated with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT, Jodhpur. Swapnil is also working closely with Dr. Rajesh Sharma, Associate Professor of the Institute of Computer Science at the University of Tartu, Estonia.

Swapnil received his master's (M.Tech) degree from the Department of Computer Engineering at the College of Engineering Pune (COEP) in 2021. He received his bachelor's (B.Tech) degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Rajarambapu Institute of Technology (RIT) Islampur, India, in 2019.

He is currently researching the domain of cyber-aggressive behavior on Indian social media.
